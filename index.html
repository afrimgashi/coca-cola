Our Proposal
A partnership built on Coca-Cola's #1 brands

We are excited to offer Gusto Italian a 5-year contract with our #1 brands, complete with exclusive pricing for all bottle-to-go ordering.

This includes a rebate offer of $2.00 on every 24ct case of bottles and every gallon of bag-in-box purchased from Coca-Cola or authorized distributor. Gusto Italian will have a volume requirement of 750 bottles and gallons annually to receive the $2.00 rebate. If Gusto Italian buys between 500–749 bottles and gallons, they will receive a $1.00 rebate.

In addition, Coca-Cola will include a $2,500 sign-on bonus to be used at your discretion. Please connect with your Account Manager for additional information regarding the best option for Gusto Italian.

On behalf of Coca-Cola, we thank you for your business and look forward to this partnership as Gusto Italian's preferred beverage brand.
